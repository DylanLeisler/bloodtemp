# Privacy Policy — BloodTemp

**Last updated:** May 14, 2026

BloodTemp is a free weather application that displays temperature in Kelvin.

## Data Collection

BloodTemp may collect your approximate device location (coarse location) when you use the "Locate" feature. This is used solely to fetch the current temperature at your location.

## How Your Data Is Used

- Your location coordinates are sent to the [OpenWeatherMap API](https://openweathermap.org/) to retrieve weather data. This is the only network request the app makes.
- City names you search are sent to OpenWeatherMap's geocoding API to resolve coordinates.
- No personal data is collected, stored on any server, or shared with any third party beyond the weather API request described above.

## Local Storage

The app stores your saved cities, home city preference, and last-viewed location locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

BloodTemp uses the OpenWeatherMap API. Their privacy policy is available at [https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy](https://openweathermap.org/privacy-policy).

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can open an issue on the project's GitHub repository at [https://github.com/dylanleisler/bloodtemp](https://github.com/dylanleisler/bloodtemp).
